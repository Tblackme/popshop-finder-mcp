About Vendor Atlas

Built to make market planning less chaotic for everyone involved.

Vendor Atlas is for artists, makers, food vendors, vintage resellers, and pop-up sellers — and for the organizers who run the markets they sell at.

Our mission

Help independent vendors spend less time digging through scattered event listings and more time choosing markets that actually fit their business. Give organizers the tools to run great events without spreadsheets and email threads.

Why it was built

Too many vendors still plan with screenshots, bookmarks, and memory. Too many organizers manage applications in email. We wanted a single workflow built around real decisions on both sides of the market.

Who it helps

Farmers market vendors, jewellery sellers, local artists, pop-up boutiques, food truck owners, ceramicists, vintage resellers — and the market organizers who give them a place to show up.

What makes it different

Four tiers built around one principle: AI is always optional. The Handcrafted tier gives you every tool with zero AI — cheaper than the AI tier on purpose. Studio connects both sides of the market in one account.

No AI required

Every AI feature has a manual version right next to it. The Handcrafted tier exists for vendors who want the full platform without any AI. That's a permanent, respected choice — not a downgrade.
