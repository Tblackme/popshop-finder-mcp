About Vendor Atlas

Built to make market planning less chaotic for working vendors.

Vendor Atlas exists for artists, makers, food vendors, vintage resellers, and pop-up sellers who need a cleaner way to discover and organize market opportunities.

Our mission

Help independent vendors spend less time digging through scattered event listings and more time choosing the markets that actually fit their business.

Why it was built

Too many vendors still plan with screenshots, bookmarks, spreadsheets, and memory. We wanted a better workflow built around real vendor decisions.

Who it helps

Farmers market vendors, jewelry sellers, local artists, pop-up boutiques, food truck owners, and creators who need a practical shortlist of where to show up next.

What makes it different

It combines discovery, event detail, and dashboard planning in one responsive experience instead of splitting them across unrelated tools.
